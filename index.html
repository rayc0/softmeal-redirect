Soft Meal

護食知識庫 · Care Food Knowledge Hub for Hong Kong

Soft Meal is an educational knowledge hub focused on dysphagia (吞嚥困難) care, IDDSI framework guidance, and texture-modified food preparation for Hong Kong caregivers, families, and care professionals.

Our forthcoming content covers safe feeding practices, IDDSI level identification, recipe preparation across all 8 IDDSI levels, caregiver training resources, and the local landscape of dysphagia services in Hong Kong.

  • Recipes and preparation guides for IDDSI Levels 3, 4, 5, 6, and 7
  • Caregiver training resources and warning signs to watch for
  • Hong Kong dysphagia services directory (speech therapists, dietitians, care homes)
  • Reference standards: T/SATA 094-2025, T/SATA 084-2025, and the international IDDSI framework
